As you go about your day, there are many things that can affect your mood. These can include how much you exercised, your interactions with others, your workday, and more. When trying to evaluate the effectiveness of an antidepressant, it's important to notice and record these external factors so that we don't confuse the effect of a drug with the effect of an external factor.

On the next page, you'll be able to input up to five external factors that you think affect your mood. You will then be asked about them when you take your daily survey. If you'd rather not add any, just click 'skip'.
